beats.bitwrap.io · feed

Curated, deterministic Petri-net beats. Every card is a content-addressed share — same URL, same audio, forever.

Card actions

▶
Play / pause this track inline.
★
Bookmark — saved locally to this browser only.
+
Add to your playlist queue.
⟳
Flag broken or stuck audio for an off-host worker to re-render. Hidden when the rebuild queue isn't enabled on this server.
genre chip
Click to filter the feed to that genre. Click again to clear.
card body
Tap (mobile) plays inline. Click (desktop) opens the synth editor with that track loaded.

Sharing

Every card URL (/?cid=…) reproduces the exact track in any browser. Append &title= to override the displayed name in the social card. Audio is rendered headless and cached on disk; a cold first listen takes 1-3 minutes.
